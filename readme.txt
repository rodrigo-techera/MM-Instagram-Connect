This Plugin Allow you to have Instagram Social Login in your site.

= Special Settings =

A section is added to your Settings menu, allowing you to configure your credentials and the styles for the button.

== Installation ==

1. Download MM Instagram Connect.
1. Upload the 'mm-instagram-connect' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
1. Activate MM Instagram Connect from your Plugins page.
1. Create an Instagram Application here <a href="https://instagram.com/developer/register/" target="_blank">https://instagram.com/developer/register/</a>.
1. Create an Instagram Client here <a href="https://instagram.com/developer/clients/register/" target="_blank">https://instagram.com/developer/clients/register/</a>.
1. Valid redirect URIs field needs to be your domain
1. Take the Client ID and Client Secret from your recent created App, and complete the fields in your settings.
